1. Introduction

Welcome to IEdit: Photo Background Remover ("IEdit", "the App", "we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application, which allows you to remove image backgrounds and replace them with solid colors or gradient backgrounds.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By downloading, installing, or using IEdit, you agree to the practices described in this document. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Images You Provide

IEdit only processes images that you explicitly select and upload within the App for background removal. These images are used solely to perform the background removal and background replacement functionality you request.

2.2 What We Do NOT Collect

We want to be fully transparent: IEdit does not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to our servers
  • Any data from your contacts, camera roll, or other device resources beyond what you explicitly select
  • Payment or financial information

3. How We Use Your Images

Images you provide to IEdit are used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To process and remove the background from your selected photo.
  • To apply your chosen solid color or gradient background to the processed image.
  • To generate and display the final edited image for you to review and export.

Image Deletion Policy

All images you upload into IEdit are processed locally on your device or temporarily in memory. Once you export or save the final edited image, all source and intermediate image data associated with that session is permanently deleted. We do not retain copies of your original photos, processed images, or any derivatives thereof.

4. Local & On-Device Processing

IEdit is designed with privacy at its core. All image processing — including background removal and background replacement — is performed locally on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party cloud service for processing.

This means:

  • Your images never leave your device during processing.
  • No internet connection is required to remove or replace a background.
  • We have no access to your images at any point.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Because we do not collect your personal data or store your images, there is nothing to share. We do not:

  • Sell, rent, or trade any user data or images to third parties.
  • Share data with advertisers, analytics providers, or marketing partners.
  • Use third-party SDKs that collect personal information.

The App does not contain advertisements or third-party tracking frameworks.

6. Data Retention

We follow a strict data minimization and no-retention policy:

  • Images uploaded for processing are deleted immediately after the edited image is exported from the App.
  • We do not maintain any database, server-side storage, or backup of user images.
  • No personal information is retained by us at any point.

7. App Permissions

IEdit may request the following iOS permissions solely to deliver its core functionality:

  • Photo Library Access: To allow you to select images from your device for background removal. We access only the specific photos you choose. We do not browse, read, or copy any other photos in your library.
  • Camera Access (if applicable): To allow you to capture a new photo directly within the App for immediate processing. Photos taken via the camera are used only for the current editing session.

You can manage and revoke these permissions at any time via your iPhone's Settings app under Privacy & Security.

8. Children's Privacy

IEdit is not directed toward children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since the App does not collect personal data from any users, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from minors. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through the App, please contact us and we will address the matter promptly.

9. Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Because all image processing occurs on-device and no personal data or images are transmitted to or stored on our servers, the privacy risk to users is minimal by design. The App does not create or maintain an account system, so there are no credentials or user profiles to protect.

We recommend that you keep your iOS device and the App updated to the latest version to benefit from the most current security protections offered by Apple and our App updates.

10. Your Rights

Since IEdit does not collect, store, or process any personal data about you beyond the transient handling of images you choose to edit, there is no personal data profile to access, correct, or delete. However, you always retain full control:

  • You decide which images to upload to the App.
  • You can stop using the App at any time.
  • You can delete the App from your device at any time, which removes all App data from your device.

If you are located in a jurisdiction with specific data protection rights (such as the EU/EEA under GDPR, or California under CCPA), you may have additional rights. Because we do not collect personal data, those rights are not practically applicable. However, you are welcome to contact us with any inquiries.
